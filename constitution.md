## Phase 1: Blueprint

### Core Problem
The current IIMBx website feels dated, flat, and generic — it fails to communicate the prestige, scale, and innovation of an IIM Bangalore digital learning initiative. The design relies on a simple WordPress-style slide hero, clip-art programme icons, heavy shadow cards with no visual energy, and zero animated statistics or social proof.

### Desired User Outcome (Golden Path)
A professional or student landing on the revamped homepage should immediately feel:
> "This is a world-class institution offering cutting-edge programmes. I want to explore and enroll."

### Non-Negotiable MVP Features
1. Premium Hero Section with gradient overlay, animated headline, dual CTA
2. Animated Stats Counter credibility wall
3. Modern Programme Cards with hover effects and programme metadata
4. Platform Partnerships Row as trust signals
5. What's New editorial card grid
6. Sticky Glassmorphism Navigation
7. Comprehensive Footer

### Design System
- Primary: Deep navy #0D1B2A | Accent: Electric blue #2563EB | Highlight: Gold #F59E0B
- Typography: Outfit (headings) + Inter (body)
- Aesthetic: Premium glassmorphism, fluid gradients, scroll animations
- Stack: Vanilla HTML + CSS + JS only
