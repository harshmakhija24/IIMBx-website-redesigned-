Who We Are

Democratizing World-Class Management Education

IIMBx, the digital learning initiative of the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, was established to provide world-class education to learners everywhere — regardless of financial, academic, or social circumstances.

Since our inception in 2014, we've pioneered the delivery of Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) through a landmark collaboration with edX, a leading non-profit learning platform founded by Harvard and MIT.

🌐
India's First Institution on edX platform
